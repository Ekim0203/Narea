About Us

↑

This website is an archive dedicated to endangered bird species in Korea. Instead of presenting extinction as a distant, heavy topic, I wanted to share it through a more familiar and approachable medium: illustration.

Each bird is more than just data — it carries a presence, a personality, and a story. Through delicate illustrations and short, emotional descriptions, this archive invites you to connect with each bird not only intellectually, but visually and emotionally.

Birds are categorized into Category I and II endangered species, and identified with red and orange bird icons. Rather than simply reading information, you are encouraged to observe each bird as you would appreciate a drawing.

This archive is not just a database — it's a quiet space for remembering lives at risk, and for fostering emotional bonds between people and nature. It will continue to grow, with plans for exhibitions, workshops, and more shared experiences.

Created by Eunseo
I love observing nature and expressing it in ways that feel warm and personal. This archive is my way of connecting with birds — and helping others do the same.
